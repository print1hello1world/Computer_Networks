The Computer Misuse Act bans unauthorised hacking and/or keylogging, installing malware, breaking into systems, and finding ways of breaking into systems intending damage. However, because this can't be verified, any offences of identity theft and gaining unauthorised access to a computer system is covered by the Act. For doing anything the Act bans can result in a fine or even going to prison.
